Privacy Policy

Snap & Learn — Last updated: March 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Snap & Learn ("the App", "we", "us", or "our"), developed by Lyanne Liang, collects, uses, and protects your personal information. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

By using Snap & Learn, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you sign in using Google Sign-In, we receive the following information from your Google account:

  • Display name
  • Email address
  • Profile photo URL (if available)

This information is used solely to identify your account and personalize your experience within the App.

1.2 Vocabulary Data

The App stores vocabulary words and associated learning data that you save, including:

  • Chinese characters, pinyin, and English definitions
  • Starred/favorited words
  • Flashcard review progress (correct/wrong counts)

1.3 Photos and Images

Important: Photos you take or upload for text recognition (OCR) are processed entirely on your device using Tesseract.js. Your images are never uploaded to any external server, cloud service, or third party. Image data remains local and is discarded after processing.

1.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • We do not use third-party analytics services
  • We do not use third-party advertising SDKs
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not collect usage telemetry or behavioral analytics
  • We do not access your contacts, microphone recordings, or files beyond the photos you explicitly provide

2. How We Use Your Information

Data Purpose Legal Basis (GDPR)
Google account info (name, email) Account authentication and personalization Contractual necessity; Legitimate interest
Vocabulary data Core app functionality — saving, reviewing, and studying vocabulary Contractual necessity
Photos (processed locally) On-device OCR to extract Chinese text Consent (you choose to take/upload a photo)

3. Data Storage and Security

Your vocabulary data and account information are stored in Firebase Firestore, a cloud database service provided by Google Cloud Platform. Firebase Firestore is governed by Google's privacy and security policies.

We implement the following security measures:

  • Firebase Security Rules restrict data access so that each user can only read and write their own data
  • All data transmission between the App and Firebase uses encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication via Google Sign-In using industry-standard OAuth 2.0

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

The only third-party service that receives your data is:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication and Firestore) — for account management and cloud data storage, governed by the Firebase Terms of Service

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Vocabulary data persists until you choose to delete individual words or your entire account.

6. Your Rights

6.1 All Users

All users of Snap & Learn have the right to:

  • Access their vocabulary data within the App at any time
  • Delete individual vocabulary words from their collection
  • Delete their account and all associated data by using the account deletion option in the App or by contacting us
  • Sign out at any time

6.2 European Economic Area (EEA) Residents — GDPR

If you are located in the EEA, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to data portability — request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to restrict processing — request that we limit how we use your data
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent — withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address below.

6.3 California Residents — CCPA

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with the following rights:

  • Right to know — what personal information is collected, used, and shared
  • Right to delete — request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to opt-out — opt out of the sale of personal information (note: we do not sell personal information)
  • Right to non-discrimination — we will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights

7. Children's Privacy

Snap & Learn is intended for general audiences and is rated "Everyone." We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete that information.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data is stored on Google Cloud servers, which may be located outside your country of residence. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to facilities operated by Google in accordance with their data protection standards.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Account and Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing us at lyanneliang@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".

Please include the email address associated with your Google account. We will permanently delete your account and all associated data (including saved vocabulary words and flashcard progress) within 30 days of receiving your request.
