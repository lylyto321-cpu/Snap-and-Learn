We're excited to introduce Snap & Learn — the fastest way to build Chinese
vocabulary from the world around you.

What's included in v1.0.0:

• Photo OCR — Take a photo or upload an image to instantly recognize Chinese
  characters using on-device text recognition. Your photos never leave your
  device.

• Visual word selection — Tap directly on recognized words overlaid on your
  photo to choose exactly which vocabulary to save.

• Comprehensive dictionary — Words are matched against CC-CEDICT with pinyin,
  English definitions, part-of-speech labels, and HSK level indicators.

• Vocabulary list — Browse, search, sort, and filter your saved words. Star
  your favorites and expand cards for detailed definitions and example
  sentences.

• Flashcard review — Study vocabulary with flip cards that track correct and
  incorrect answers. Switch between Chinese-to-English and English-to-Chinese
  modes.

• Audio pronunciation — Tap to hear native Mandarin pronunciation on vocabulary
  cards and during flashcard review.

• Example sentences — View contextual example sentences with pinyin and English
  translations for deeper understanding.

• Google Sign-In — Sign in securely with your Google account to sync vocabulary
  across devices via Firebase.

• Privacy-first design — All image processing happens on-device. No analytics.
  No ads. No tracking.
