FULL DESCRIPTION (4000 characters max)
---------------------------------------
Finally, a Chinese learning app that works the way real life does.

Forget drilling textbook word lists. Snap & Learn lets you learn Chinese from the world around you — menus, street signs, packaging, books, anywhere you see Chinese characters. Just take a photo, tap the words you want to learn, and they're instantly saved to your personal flashcard deck. That's it.

Millions of people are trying to learn Chinese but giving up because traditional apps feel disconnected from real life. Snap & Learn bridges that gap — turning every moment you encounter Chinese text into a learning opportunity.

WHY SNAP & LEARN?
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

✦ REAL-WORLD VOCABULARY
Don't study words you'll never use. Learn the words you actually encounter every day — from menus, signs, packaging, and anything else in your environment. Context makes words stick.

✦ INSTANT OCR — NO TYPING EVER
Our on-device text recognition (powered by Tesseract.js) reads Chinese characters from photos instantly. No internet needed for scanning. Your photos never leave your device — complete privacy.

✦ 100,000+ WORD DICTIONARY
Every word is looked up in CC-CEDICT, one of the world's most comprehensive Chinese-English dictionaries. Get pinyin pronunciation, multiple definitions, part-of-speech, and HSK level — everything you need in one tap.

✦ SMART FLASHCARDS THAT ADAPT
Our flashcard system remembers which words trip you up and prioritizes them. Study Chinese→English or flip to English→Chinese. The more you practice, the smarter your deck gets.

✦ HEAR IT SPOKEN INSTANTLY
Tap any word to hear it pronounced clearly. Perfect for getting tones right — the part of Chinese that trips up most learners.

✦ EXAMPLE SENTENCES IN CONTEXT
See how each word is used in real sentences, with pinyin and English translations. Understanding words in context is how fluency actually develops.

✦ YOUR WORDS, ALWAYS WITH YOU
Sign in with Google and your entire vocabulary list — words, stars, flashcard scores — syncs instantly across all your devices. Never lose your progress.

✦ BUILT FOR YOUR PRIVACY
Photos processed 100% on your device. Nothing uploaded. Your data is yours.
